# Code of Conduct

Project Jubilee 50 contributors are expected to keep the project welcoming, practical, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent, then clarify.
- Keep feedback about the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy around personal story material and media in the game.
- Help new contributors find the right docs, files, and tests.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private personal information.
- Pressuring people to share private context, photos, or recordings.
- Adding assets or references that violate the asset policy.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the maintainers through GitHub. If the issue involves private information, avoid posting sensitive details publicly; ask for a private maintainer contact first.
